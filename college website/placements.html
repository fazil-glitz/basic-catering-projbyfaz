At CAHCET College of Engineering and Technology (ECET), we pride ourselves on offering excellent placement opportunities for our students. Our dedicated Training and Placement Cell works tirelessly to bridge the gap between students and industry leaders, ensuring that our graduates are not only academically prepared but also industry-ready. Our Placement Process: Training Programs: We offer extensive training sessions that focus on soft skills, technical skills, resume writing, and interview preparation. Our goal is to equip students with the skills necessary to succeed in the professional world. Internships: Through our strong industry connections, students are provided with internship opportunities to gain hands-on experience during their course of study. These internships often act as a prelude to full-time employment. Campus Recruitment Drives: Each year, we host on-campus recruitment drives where top companies from various sectors visit our campus to recruit talented graduates. We also organize off-campus recruitment events for specific industries. Career Guidance: Our placement cell provides personalized career counseling and mentorship, helping students choose the right career path based on their strengths, interests, and industry trends. Our Recruiters: Over the years, ECET has developed strong relationships with leading companies across various industries, including technology, manufacturing, consulting, and finance.
